INDIAN CRICKETER ROHIT SHARMA

Introduction

Rohit Sharma, popularly known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, is one of the most admired cricketers in the world. Born on 30th April 1987 in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra, Rohit has inspired millions with his incredible talent, calm attitude, and leadership skills. His journey from humble beginnings to becoming the captain of the Indian cricket team in various formats is truly motivating.
